11. Shipping & Delivery

 

We strive to provide customers with the best dental products. After each order is placed with us, we have a staff members fill orders within 1-3 business days. All orders will arrive 7-10 days (depending on shipping option) after your order has been placed.

 

All of our products will be shipped through USPS. A shipping calculator is displayed during checkout with different shipping options USPS offers and the price for each option. After your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with details on your shipment.

 

All of our shipments come with USPS Delivery Confirmation. This service allows you to always know where your package is and also lets us know when we can complete your order in our records. Tracking numbers can be found on each order listed in the “My Account” area or in your shipment notification email.
